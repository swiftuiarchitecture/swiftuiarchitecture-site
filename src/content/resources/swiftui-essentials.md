This WWDC session introduces the core principles of SwiftUI, including declarative UI development, view composition, state management with `@State` and `@Binding`, custom views, animations, adaptive controls, multi-platform development, and interoperability with UIKit, AppKit, SwiftData, and other Apple frameworks.
